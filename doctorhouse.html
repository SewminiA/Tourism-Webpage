The Doctor’s House is a 200-year-old former Dutch Ayurvedic hospital, situated on the south coast of Sri Lanka in the beautiful beach side village of Madiha. In 2016 it was transformed into a bar, restaurant, shop and hotel by couple of Australian guys, whom openly admit to having zero hospitality experience! They simply aim to please customers by creating a good vibe and having a bit of fun.
